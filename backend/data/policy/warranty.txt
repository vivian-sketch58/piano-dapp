BLUEROSEMART WARRANTY POLICY
=============================

1. PLATFORM WARRANTY DISCLAIMER
BlueRoseMart is a peer-to-peer blockchain marketplace. The platform does not provide any warranty on second-hand pianos listed by sellers. All warranty claims must be directed to the seller or the original piano manufacturer.

2. SELLER WARRANTY OBLIGATIONS
Sellers are encouraged to disclose any existing warranty on the piano in the listing description. If a seller states a warranty in their listing, they are legally obligated to honour it. BlueRoseMart may take action against sellers who misrepresent warranty status.

3. MANUFACTURER WARRANTY
Some pianos may still be within the original manufacturer warranty period. Sellers should state this clearly in the listing. Buyers should verify warranty transferability with the manufacturer before purchasing, as some warranties are non-transferable.

4. COMMON MANUFACTURER WARRANTY PERIODS
- Yamaha acoustic pianos: 5 years parts and labour for original owner
- Kawai acoustic pianos: 10 years for the original owner
- Steinway pianos: 5 years limited warranty
- Roland digital pianos: 2 years parts and labour
- Casio digital pianos: 1 year limited warranty
Note: Warranty terms vary by model and region. Always verify with the manufacturer directly.

5. SECOND-HAND WARRANTY CONSIDERATIONS
Most manufacturer warranties are voided or non-transferable on second-hand sales. Buyers of second-hand pianos should:
- Ask the seller for all original documentation and receipts
- Contact the manufacturer to verify warranty transferability
- Consider purchasing an extended warranty from a third-party provider
- Budget for potential repairs given the piano's age and condition

6. WHAT IS NOT COVERED
BlueRoseMart does not cover the following under any warranty:
- Normal wear and tear including key discoloration and felt wear
- Damage caused by improper moving or storage
- Damage from humidity or temperature extremes
- Modifications or repairs by non-authorised technicians
- Cosmetic damage described in the original listing

7. PIANO SERVICING RECOMMENDATION
BlueRoseMart recommends all buyers have their purchased piano inspected and serviced by a certified piano technician within 30 days of delivery. This ensures any pre-existing issues are documented and the piano is in optimal playing condition.

8. DISPUTES RELATED TO WARRANTY
If a seller falsely claims warranty coverage, buyers may raise a dispute with BlueRoseMart. Provide evidence of the misrepresentation and the dispute will be reviewed within 3 to 7 business days.
